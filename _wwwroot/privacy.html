Welcome to SentinelPC. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Information We Collect

SentinelPC is designed to operate primarily locally on your device. We strive to minimize data collection. The information we may collect includes:

  • System Information: To provide monitoring and optimization features, the Application may access information about your computer's hardware (CPU, RAM, disk), operating system, and running processes. This information is processed locally and is generally not transmitted to us unless explicitly required for troubleshooting or diagnostics (with your consent).
  • Application Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with the Application (e.g., features used, frequency of use, performance metrics, errors encountered). This helps us understand usage patterns, improve the Application, and identify bugs. This data is aggregated and anonymized and does not personally identify you.
  • Backup Data: When using the backup feature, the Application will access the files and folders you designate for backup. These backups are stored locally on your device or on storage locations you configure (e.g., external drives, network locations). SentinelPC does not automatically transmit your backup data to our servers or any third party. You are solely responsible for the security and management of your backup storage locations.
  • Diagnostic and Crash Reports: If the Application encounters an error, you may be prompted to send a diagnostic report to us. These reports may contain system information, application state, and error details necessary to diagnose the problem. We will seek your consent before sending any such report.

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number through the standard operation of the Application unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., when contacting support).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected primarily to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Application's features (monitoring, optimization, backups).
  • Improve, personalize, and expand the Application.
  • Understand and analyze how you use the Application.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • Identify and fix bugs and errors.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the Application, and for marketing and promotional purposes (where applicable and consented to).
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the Application.

  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

Since SentinelPC primarily processes data locally and collects minimal personal data, exercising these rights may often involve managing the data directly on your device or contacting us regarding any anonymously collected usage data or diagnostic reports you may have sent.
